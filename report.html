1. Strategic Diagnosis

1.1 The Core Problem

ElectricPe has a measurement and distribution problem, not a product problem. The business model works — 6,000+ EVs sold, 4.4-rated app, Google Maps partnership. But the digital infrastructure is broken at every layer. This creates four structural vulnerabilities:

  • Blind operations: No Google Tag Manager installed. No GA4. No data layer. 0 of 22 tracking checks passed. Every tracking change requires a developer deploy (3-7 days vs. minutes with GTM). The test ride booking — ElectricPe's most important conversion event — has zero connection to any analytics or advertising platform.
  • Zero paid acquisition: No paid advertising exists on any platform. Google Ads Transparency Center and Meta Ad Library show nothing. While competitors spend aggressively, ElectricPe has no scalable digital acquisition channel.

2. SEO Audit — Score: 38/100

2.1 The Three Most Damaging SEO Issues

Issue #1: Missing XML Sitemap. The /sitemap.xml returns empty or no data. This is the single most impactful technical SEO failure. With 15,000+ charging stations, dozens of city pages, and blog posts, ElectricPe potentially has thousands of indexable URLs that Google simply cannot discover. Only ~15 pages are indexed when 500-2,000+ should be.

Issue #2: 3-Way URL Cannibalization. Three separate URLs compete for "EV charging stations" — /evchargingstations/, /ev-charging-station/, and /ev-charging-stations/. Google cannot determine which to rank. Ranking signals are split three ways. None rank well.

Issue #3: Thin, JavaScript-Dependent Content. Critical pages render primarily through client-side JavaScript. The EV Charging Stations page shows "content appears to be mostly CSS/JS" to crawlers. Googlebot may see little to no indexable content on key money pages.

The Rs 299 test ride is ElectricPe's most powerful conversion mechanism. A customer who takes a test ride is estimated to be 10x more likely to purchase an EV. Yet this critical event has zero connection to any advertising measurement.

6.2 Privacy & Consent

India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA) 2023 requires clear notice before collecting personal data and consent before processing for marketing. ElectricPe currently has no visible consent mechanism for tracking technologies. With investors like Blume Ventures and Green Frontier Capital on the cap table, this is the kind of compliance risk that comes up during due diligence.

Recommended consent stack: GTM Consent Mode v2 with CookieYes or OneTrust (free tier). Default: denied for analytics/ad storage. On accept: grant all consent signals. On decline: GA4 fires with cookieless pings. Google Ads consent mode modeling recovers ~70% of declined-consent conversions.
